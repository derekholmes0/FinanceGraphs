# Constant Maturity UST rates

FRED calculated constant maturity interest rates

## Usage

``` r
yc_CMSUST
```

## Format

### `yc_CMSUST`

A long format `data.table` with constant maturity UST with tenors 2
year, 10 year and 30 year

- variable:

  Term of UST

- date:

  Date of observation

- value:

  Annualized Percent interest rae

## Source

<https://fred.stlouisfed.org/>
